Grapple Hook

Grapple Hook is a rage game with simple graphics where you control a grappling hook to make your way through the levels. Each level takes anywhere from 50 to 10 minutes to play, but you can save your progress and come back later. (Data only exists on the device that you saved it on)

The End of the World

The End of the World is a game where you fight endless waves of enemies with just a sword. An AI decides to help you, and can spawn walls to protect you from the enemy attacks. It also sells you things that make you stronger. This game was made in 3 days for the Wowie Game Jam.

Escape

A short story-based game made up of background images, and dialouge from the other character. You can change the outcome of the game by either doing what they say or doing the opposite. There are several different endings and you get different dialouge when reaching an ending multiple times. This game was made in one week for the Brackeys Game Jam.

Flippy Bard

One of my first polished games, Flippy Bard is a Flappy Bird game where you control William Shakespeare's head , which flips every time you click or press space. This game is also on the Google Play Store for Android devices.
